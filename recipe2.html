Chicken Divan

Despite the name, chicken Divan is not a classic French dish. Rather it was created at the Divan Parisien Restaurant at the Chatham Hotel in New York City which also has been credited with introducing Americans to broccoli. There is just no exact date when the dish was actually created as some sources say in the 1930s while others say the 1940s.

Well first off, the dish is chicken and broccoli slathered in a white sauce topped with delicious Parmesan cheese to create a dish that is oddly comforting for something that was served at fancy events. If you are planning a fifties dinner party, then I would give this dish a try. I was surprised how quickly and easily it comes together and still looks pretty with very little effort.

Recipe

This Chicken Divan has juicy bites of chicken that are smothered in a simple cream sauce with broccoli and topped with a buttery Ritz cracker topping. This is a quick family dinner that you can serve with rice, mashed potatoes, or over biscuits.

Ingrediants

  • 4 C broccoli, cooked and chopped
  • 2 lbs chicken breasts, diced rotisserie or cooked
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1 C mayonnaise
  • 1 T lemon juice (fresh is my choice)
  • 1 t curry
  • 1/2–1 C cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/2 C bread crumbs
  • 2 T butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°.
  2. Measure all ingredients.
  3. Butter or spray with cooking spray a 9 x 13 baking dish.
  4. Cook broccoli and drain. Chop and add broccoli to the baking dish in a layer on the bottom.
  5. Dice cooked or rotisserie chicken and add to mixing bowl.
  6. Add soup, curry, mayonnaise and lemon juice to the cooked chicken and mix well.
  7. Pour chicken mixture on top of broccoli in the baking dish.
  8. Cover the top of the chicken mixture with an even layer of cheese.
  9. Melt butter and mix together with the bread crumbs. Sprinkle over the top of the casserole.
  10. Add baking dish to a preheated 325° oven and bake for 45–60 minutes until the mixture is bubbly and topping golden brown.
  11. Serve immediately.
